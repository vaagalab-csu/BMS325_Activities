2. GPCR Cascades & Signal Amplification

Explore how a single ligand-bound GPCR can be amplified into many second messengers and phosphorylated targets. Move the slider to step through the cascade.

Metabotropic receptors ("GPCRs") couple ligand binding to intracellular cascades: GPCR → G-protein → enzyme (e.g., adenylyl cyclase) → second messenger (cAMP, IP3, DAG) → kinases → many target proteins. Each step can amplify the signal.
